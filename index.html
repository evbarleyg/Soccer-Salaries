Intent

This dashboard is designed to benchmark club spend across Premier League and LaLiga fairly by combining wage bill and transfer activity in one comparable surface.

Structure

  • Filters: clubs, season, currency, sort, spend view.
  • Overview cards: total spend, wages, net transfers, contract coverage.
  • Club table: sortable spend drivers by club.
  • Player drilldown: incoming and outgoing deals per selected team.
  • Data quality: coverage and assumption pressure at club level.

How To Use

  1. Select clubs from the multi-select dropdown and pick season/spend view.
  2. Sort columns in Multi-League Club Spend Table to rank key spend drivers.
  3. Use Player Drilldown to inspect individual transfer assumptions and confidence.
  4. Review Data Quality to understand where assumptions may affect conclusions.
